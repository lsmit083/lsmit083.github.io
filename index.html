ABOUT LAURA ROSE INTERIORS

LAURA ROSE INTERIORS offers design services for a full range of possibilities, ranging from small bathroom renovations to entire home remodels. 

Laura Rose graduated from Bob Jones University in December of 2026 with her bachelor's in Interior Architecture and Design. She furthered her studies by attending the University of Cincinnati's architectural program, receiving her masters in architecture. 

Laura Rose Interior's purpose is to make  others feel seen, heard, and valued. Our mission is to design a space that you will not only live in, but will thrive.
